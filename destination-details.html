Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know for your Santorini adventure

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Paris is from April to June or September to October when the weather is pleasant, and the city is less crowded.

Weather & Climate

Paris has a temperate oceanic climate with mild winters (around 40-45°F / 5-7°C) and warm summers (70-80°F / 21-27°C). Rain is spread throughout the year.

Currency & Language

Euro (EUR) is the official currency. French is the primary language, and English is widely spoken in tourist areas.

Visa Requirements

EU citizens need only an ID card. Citizens of the US, Canada, and Australia require a valid passport for stays up to 90 days without a visa.

Local Tips & Cultural Insights

  • Book museum and restaurant reservations well in advance
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes for cobblestone streets
  • Carry a reusable water bottle and stay hydrated
  • Respect local customs, especially in churches and historic sites
  • Try local specialties: croissants, escargot, and French wine
  • Use the Metro and buses for convenient city travel
